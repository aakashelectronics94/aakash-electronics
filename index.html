OUR STORY

Thirty years on SP Road.

Founded in 1994 by Mr. Kiran Kumar. Still run by him. Still on the same counter.

Mr. Kiran Kumar started on SP Road in 1994 as a retail counter for passive components and ICs. A year later, word had spread to the industrial belt manufacturing units and government organisations started placing bulk orders, and a small shop became a trusted procurement partner across Karnataka.

Established as Aakash Electronics in 1994, the shop grew quickly. Clients like L&T, BEL, and Indian Railways became regulars. The range of customers expanded, but the philosophy never changed: stock the right parts, charge a fair price, and treat every question, whether for a thousand unit order or a single resistor, with the same care.

Through the 2000s, as Bengaluru's infrastructure expanded, Aakash added a full range of wires and cables for contractors, integrators, and industrial electricians. Then came engineering students, and the radio assembly kit became one of the top-selling educational products in the region.

Today, over thirty years later, Mr. Kiran Kumar still runs the shop from the same SP Road counter. One of the widest component selections on SP Road. Honest pricing. And the same willingness to explain a datasheet as many times as it takes.

FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

Everything you'd want to know before you visit or call.

Do you sell online or ship orders outside Bengaluru?

We are a walk-in counter, no online store, no website orders. For large B2B orders outside Bengaluru, contact us and we can discuss options. For most orders, customers visit us directly at SP Road.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No minimum at all. One resistor, one sensor, one kit every order is welcome. Bulk orders get volume pricing; just ask at the counter or WhatsApp us for a quote.

Do you issue GST invoices?

Yes. We are a GST-registered business and issue proper tax invoices for every purchase. For B2B input credit, please provide your GSTIN before the invoice is raised, as we cannot amend invoices once they have been raised. Interstate purchases attract IGST.

How do I find out if you stock a specific component?

The fastest way is to WhatsApp us or call +91 99800 22755 with your part number, description, or datasheet link. We'll confirm availability and pricing promptly.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, UPI, and bank transfer (NEFT / IMPS). Retail and one-off purchases are settled at the time of transaction. For established B2B accounts, payment terms can be discussed and mutually agreed upon. We understand that businesses operate on billing cycles and are happy to work something out. Please speak with us directly to arrange this.

How do I get to SP Road?

We're at #84/4, 1st Floor, N.G.M Plaza, S.P Road, Bengaluru – 560002. KR Market metro station (Green Line) is a 10-minute walk. Town Hall bus stop is about 5 minutes away. Two-wheelers are easiest for parking.
